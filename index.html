About Us


Dumplins Bistro and Bakery was first founded and 1990 and has gained popularity throughout the years. Our team works together to ensure the best hospitality and experience at Dumplins. We offer catering, to-go, dining and delivery services for special events and everyday needs. You can reserve one of our dining areas to host extravagant parties or events. We have a variety of cakes and desserts for the whole family. We also design cakes for your individual needs!
